University Of Petra

University of Petra (UOP) is a private university in Amman, Jordan. The University offers the Bachelor and Master Program, and it has more than 33 nationalities from Arab and foreign countries. The University is located on the airport road and features a green campus as well as facilities such as workplaces, laboratories and studios. It offers an applied education.
